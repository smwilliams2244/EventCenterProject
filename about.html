Our Spaces

Grand Hall

A dramatic, light-filled event space with soaring ceilings, retractable glass walls, and adaptable lighting, designed to transform seamlessly from elegant weddings to polished corporate galas.

Atrium Lounge

An indoor-outdoor lounge featuring a living plant wall, statement bar, and modular seating, perfect for cocktail hours, networking events, and relaxed social gatherings.

Studio Wing

Three flexible, soundproof studios with sliding partitions, ideal for wellness workshops, breakout sessions, bridal suites, creative meetings, or private preparation spaces.

Courtyard Garden

An intimate outdoor courtyard with string lights, fire pits, water feature, and ceremony arch, creating a romantic backdrop for sunset ceremonies and evening celebrations.
